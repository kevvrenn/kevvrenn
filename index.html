About Hinata

Shoyo Hinata is the main protagonist of the anime “Haikyuu!!” that came out on April 6 2014. For the majority of the show, Hinata was a student at Karasuno High and one of the volleyball team’s middle blockers. He enrolled into Karasuno in April 2012 and brought their volleyball team back to glory with his teammates after multiple matches against strong competitors.

Appearance

Around his first year of high school Hinata stood at 162.8 cm or 5’ 4 and he grew to 164.2 cm halfway through the school year. He is the second shortest member of the Karasuno team next to Nishinoya. He has brown eyes and messy orange hair. During matches Hinata wears his uniform with his position number 10, the same number worn by the Small Giant.

Personality

Hinata has always had a fun, cheerful, and energetic personality. He is also very simple-minded for the most part which causes him to more than occasionally make rash decisions, but he is also shown to be quite observant at times, being able to read social cues and show empathy towards others. Though seeming lighthearted he is often shown to be quite serious when it comes to volleyball, his love for the sport can be clearly seen through his desire to improve his abilities.
